Editorial Standards

The platform exists to test arguments. To protect debate quality:

  • Attack ideas, not people. Personal abuse is not allowed.
  • No incitement or threats.
  • No doxxing or sharing private information.
  • Stay on topic. Argue the issue being debated.
  • Reasoning over slogans. Unsupported claims may be challenged or moderated.

Two positions are presented for each issue. Those positions may be created using a mix of human and AI assistance, and are intended as structured prompts for debate — not as endorsements.
