Methodology

Momentum (Velocity)

Momentum reflects recent activity. It is driven by new comments within a rolling time window and decays frequently so the homepage stays fresh.

Heat

Heat reflects intensity. It increases with participation and reply depth. Heat is slower-moving than momentum.

Debate lifecycle

  • Active: open to comments.
  • Frozen: comments closed to preserve the record.
  • Archived: searchable, indexable, permanently closed.

Lifecycle timing is automated and may be adjusted as the platform evolves.
